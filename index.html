Our Story

Built in Abuja.
Built for the World.

Kace Technologies was founded in 2018 by engineers frustrated with the gap between the software Nigerian businesses deserved and what was available locally. We set out to change that.

Who We Are

We're a 35-person team of engineers, designers, and strategists who believe Africa's digital economy deserves world-class software. Our team has worked across fintech, healthcare, logistics, and enterprise platforms — bringing that depth to every engagement.

We operate from Gwarinpa, Abuja — but our clients span Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, UK, and the US. We're proud to be Nigeria-based and globally competitive.

Our Journey

2018
Kace Technologies Founded
Started by 4 engineers in Wuse 2, Abuja — with a focus on fintech solutions.
2019
First Enterprise Client
Landed our first major enterprise contract delivering a custom ERP integration for a Lagos logistics firm.
2021
Team Expansion & New HQ
Grew to 20 engineers and moved to our current Gwarinpa office. Launched our mobile app practice.
2023
120+ Projects Delivered
Crossed 120 projects across 9 industries, with clients in 4 countries.
2025
Expanding Across Africa
Opening a second office in Lagos and launching our Cloud Centre of Excellence practice.
